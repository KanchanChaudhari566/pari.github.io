Introduction of the Institute with the Mission Statement and Objective

A. History

GURUKUL PRIVATE ITI has been setup with a view to ensure a steady flow of skilled personnel and to reduce unemployment amongst the educated youth by equipping them with required skills, education and discipline for suitable industrial employment as well as self-employment. They impart training in engineering trades as per the syllabus and curriculum approved by Government of India. Presently it offers training in 2 trades. Each trade has its own lab/workshop, which is well equipped with latest equipment’s and High technically educated instructor’s team. GURUKUL PRIVATE ITI provides semi skilled/ skilled workers to industry by systematic training to school leavers. It reduces unemployment among educated youth by equipping them with suitable skills for industrial employment.
